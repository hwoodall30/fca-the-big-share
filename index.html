The Big Share is a giving initiative that brings together local nonprofit ministries focused on reaching the next generation. This season, we're excited to collaborate with 5 ministries across our county to amplify our collective impact. Your one gift helps support each of these ministries, funding scholarships, events, camps, and more–it’s one Big Share!
When we share, we grow—and when we grow together, we build a stronger, more vibrant future for the next generation. Join us in The Big Share!
